About Wantex Insurance

Your trusted insurance partner, committed to protecting what matters most to you

Who We Are

Wantex Insurance Agency is a leading insurance intermediary in Kenya, dedicated to providing comprehensive and affordable insurance solutions for individuals, families, and businesses. We bridge the gap between our clients and reputable insurance underwriters, ensuring you get the best coverage at the most competitive rates.

Founded on the principles of trust, transparency, and exceptional customer service, Wantex Insurance has grown to become a preferred insurance partner for thousands of Kenyans. We understand that every client has unique needs, which is why we take a personalized approach to insurance — carefully assessing your risks and recommending tailored solutions that give you true peace of mind.

Our team of experienced and licensed insurance professionals brings together decades of combined expertise across all classes of insurance. From motor and health to property, business, and marine insurance, we offer end-to-end support — from policy placement and premium negotiation to claims management and advisory services.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of insurance does Wantex offer?
We offer a comprehensive range of insurance products including Motor Insurance (comprehensive & third party), Health Insurance (individual, family & corporate), Home & Property Insurance, Travel Insurance, Personal Accident Cover, Fire Insurance, Theft & Burglary, Liability Insurance, Marine Insurance, and other specialized commercial covers.
How do I get an insurance quote from Wantex?
Getting a quote is easy! You can fill out our online quote request form on the Contact page, call us directly at +254 700 000 000, email us at info@wantex.co.ke, or visit our office in Nairobi. Our team will assess your needs and provide you with competitive quotes from multiple underwriters within 24 hours.
How does the claims process work?
When you need to make a claim, simply contact us and we'll guide you through the entire process. We help with documentation, liaison with the insurance company, and follow up until settlement. Our dedicated claims team ensures a smooth and hassle-free experience, with most claims processed efficiently.
Which insurance companies does Wantex work with?
We partner with over 15 of Kenya's top-rated insurance underwriters. This allows us to compare products and premiums across multiple providers, ensuring you get the best coverage at the most competitive price. All our partner companies are licensed by the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA) of Kenya.
Can I customize my insurance policy?
Absolutely! We believe that one size does not fit all when it comes to insurance. Our advisors work closely with you to understand your specific needs and risks, then tailor a policy that provides the right level of coverage. We can add riders, adjust limits, and bundle policies to give you optimal protection.
Is Wantex Insurance a licensed agency?
Yes, Wantex Insurance Agency is fully licensed and regulated by the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA) of Kenya. We operate in full compliance with the Insurance Act and all relevant regulations, ensuring your interests are always protected.
