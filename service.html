Bemih Kanyonge

Managing Partner, Advocate

Biography

Bemih is an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya. He has experience working in civil society, private practice and public sector. He has extensive experience in land transactions & dispute, construction disputes resolution, commercial and tax advisory and litigation, civil and criminal litigation and has been involved in public interest litigation. He has also appeared before tribunals most notably The Sports Tribunal, The Tax Appeals Tribunal, and the Business Premises Tribunal etc.

He has previously consulted for and worked with Trade Mark Africa (TMA), State Department for East African Affairs, Amnesty International, Transparency International-Kenya, Legal Resources Foundation, East Africa Centre for Human Rights, Nairobi City County, Muthaura Mugambi Ayugi & Njonjo (MMAN) Advocates, Kinuthia Kahindi & Co. Advocates, Kituo cha Sheria and The Open Society Foundations, Budapest, Hungary – an international non-governmental organisation. Bemih has over 15 years work experience.
